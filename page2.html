Cat Adoption within the UK

Why adopt rather than buy?

  • The charity will be aware of each cat's personality and needs and can appropriately match you
  • You will not be directly supporting the currently unregulated cat breeding practices within the UK, which so often results in cats in ill health
  • Adult cats will already be neutered/spayed and have had a full health check prior to adoption
  • You will be directly supporting animal rescue in the wider community (e.g. Trap, Neuter, Return of feral cats)
